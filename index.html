What Quick Cleats Does

Quick Cleats are low-profile, thermoformed turf molds that snap securely over metal-spiked baseball cleats to convert them instantly into turf-safe shoes. Designed for in-game use on artificial turf, they preserve spike performance while protecting turf and eliminating shoe changes between innings or surfaces.

Fast Snap-Lock

Clip-in tabs hold the cover firmly during sprints and lateral cuts — remove in under 12 seconds.

Turf-Optimized Traction

Non-marking nubs provide grip on artificial grass without tearing or hooking.

Durability

High-durometer TPU withstands repeated cleat contact and abrasion from turf infill.

Low Profile

Keeps player height and balance nearly identical to naked cleats — designed for minimal impact on stride and swing mechanics.

How to Use

  1. Clip the Quick Cleats over your metal cleat (toe first).
  2. Snap the rear tabs into place — do a quick tug test.
  3. Play on turf as usual. Remove when returning to dirt/grass or indoors.

Who It's For

High school & college teams that split surfaces, travel ball programs, catchers & pitchers who move between bullpen and turf, and coaches who want a one-cleat solution.
